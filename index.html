Features of Visfolio

Visfolio is a cutting-edge data visualization system designed for the financial market. Our platform provides powerful tools to help both novice and seasoned investors make informed decisions by visualizing financial data in an intuitive way.

  • Financial Text Mining: Unlock insights from financial texts with our advanced text mining tools.
  • Global Market Visualization: Get a bird's-eye view of major stock markets through interactive Treemaps and Geographic Maps.
  • Correlation Analysis: Calculate and visualize the correlation between stocks or other financial instruments to manage and reduce portfolio risks.
  • Backtest Performance: Review the historical performance of your portfolio and simulate different investment strategies.
